What is Acne ?
Acne is the overactivity of oil glands in the skin. This leads to increased production of sebum in the gland. Eventually this leads to the formation of acne.

Why do acne usually occur ?
Acne, mostly occur during times of puberty when not only there is increased activity of oil glands, but also hormonal changes that lead to acne. There are other factors also that lead to acne in both teenagers and adults. Visit us for Best Acne treatment.
Acne can appear as :
1) Blackheads.
2) Whiteheads.
3) Pustules that are pus filled.
4) Nodules that are firm to hard when you feel them.
5) Cystic that appear as sac like eruptions under the surface of the skin.

What is Adult Acne ?
Adult acne is also a very common concern. It can happen due to various reasons. One of the most common reason is loss of oil water balance of the skin That causes oil glands to become overactive our skin has a very delicate oil water balance. There is constant loss of water happening through the surface of the skin with this excessive loss when not compensated leads to overactivity of oil glands for the purpose to prevent more water loss from skin surface. Think of it as if you apply a layer of oil on a wilting flower This will prevent loss of remaining water from the surface.

OTHER CAUSES THAT CAN AGGRAVATE ACNE
Acne can be aggravated or irritated by various other factors like
PCOS / PCOD - Polycystic ovary disease one of the most common cause of acne in present-day life Though it is very common. It is easily manageable.
Hormonal issues - Various hormonal distances in Hormones like thyroid Female sex hormones male sex hormones can cause acne .
Stress - Stress is also one of the main factors causing acne constant levels of increased stress can lead to increased levels of Cortisol in the body leading to acne.
Miscellaneous Causes – Various other Causes have been attributed to acne like digestive issues improper eating habits intake of excessively oily Foods etc.
