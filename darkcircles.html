Why do under eye dark circles develop ?
Under eye dark circles developed basically of three reasons.
1) Excessive pigmentation - This means increased pigmentation around the area of eye due to various reasons.
2) Excessive vascularity - As eyelid is very thin the blood vessels present within the muscles are easily visualized the voices changes lead to appearance of Dark circles.
3) Shadow effect - A combination of loose skin and appearance of fat from breed The muscle leads to a shadow effect that looks like dark circles.
