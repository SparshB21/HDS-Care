SYMPTOMS OF GUM DISEASE
Gum diseases begin with certain symptoms which we shouldn’t ignore.
a)Red or swollen gums.
b)Constant bad breath.
c)Bleeding gums.
d)Loose teeth.
e)Painful chewing.
f)Sensitive teeth.

1. Gingivitis
In Gingivitis disease, the gums become red, swollen and bleed, where this mainly occurs due to inadequate oral hygiene. We find that this cause little or no discomfort at an early stage and can be reversed by taking proper treatment and oral home care. 2. Periodontitis
Gum disease can reach up to the level of pyorrhoea where bacteria in the plaque region may cause the production of toxins. These toxins can irritate the gums, tissues, as well as bones which basically support the teeth. This tooth gets broken down and destroyed. This one of the most effective ways to treat gum disease. In this procedure, gums are lifted back and plaque is removed. It works better for smoothening of damaged bone & to restrict the area disease-causing bacteria. The gums are placed well-positioned.

This procedure is very effective in the treatment of destroyed bone caused by gum disease. The bone graft procedure is the best for the regrowth of bone and helps in restoring the teeth’s stability.
