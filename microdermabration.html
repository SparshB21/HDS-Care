Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion Or MDA As it mostly called is the method of skin resurfacing. It is one of the most popular methods for skin Rejuvenation today due to minimal downtime and low incidence of side effects.

What are the types of microdermabrasion available ?
Mostly two types of microdermabrasion options are available.

Is micro Derma abrasion and Derma abrasion same ?
No, microdermabrasion and dermabrasion are not same in microdermabrasion is on only the Superficial layer of epidermis is removed. Whereas in dermabrasion. The entire thickness of skin is removed using Dermabraders. Microdermabrasion is a milder process but benefits are numerous.

Is there any down time in MDA ?
Usually MD does not have any downtime. There is no momentary redness during the procedure that usually subsides with 15 20 minutes. Essentially. It is a lunchtime Procedure.

What are the precautions you should take post MDA treatment ?
MDA has no down time and requires minimal care routine post-procedure precautions are as follows:
1) Apply sunscreen immediately after the procedure and do not miss your sunscreen to prevent from sun damage.
2) Avoid using creams containing steroids two to three days after the treatment.
3) Avoid using fragrance containing products on skin for two days after the treatment.
4) Keep 10- 14 days interval between session to allow complete regeneration.
