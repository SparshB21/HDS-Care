TYPES OF TEETH POLISHING
There are different types of tooth polishing procedures including – Therapeutic polishing, coronal/ cosmetic polishing, superficial and selective polishing. Let’s have a quick overview of tooth polishing.
1) Therapeutic Polishing - It’s a dental surgical procedure where its prime objective is to remove the microflora and endotoxins from the cementum surface.
2) Coronal/cosmetic polishing - This polishing is very effective in plaque eradication and dental stains where it works great in order to make enamel lustrous. The enamel surface is polished to a greater degree.
3) Superficial polishing - In dental treatment, this technique is widely used in polishing the crown of the teeth. It improves the appearance of the teeth.
4) Selective Polishing - This dental treatment works fine to treat the extrinsic stain and selective stain. The tooth polishing is quite good in removing both types of stains including – endogenous and exogenous stains. Where the first one is linked with the internal & the next one with external stains.
Generally, internal stains are developed at the tooth development stage, on the other hand, external ones after the tooth has erupted. Smoking and poor dental habits are the prime reasons for external stains. Tooth polishing is considered best for removing the exogenous stains, not the internal one.
