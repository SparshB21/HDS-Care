WHAT EXACTLY IS VAMPIRE FACIAL?
1) Vampire facial is one of the finest approaches to improve the youthful appearance of the skin. In this, a sample of blood is taken out from the face skin.
2) After this plasma is extracted and injected back into the skin.
3) The purpose of injecting plasma is to stimulate collagen growth and healthy skin.
4) The plasma has platelets, growth factors, and other nutrients therefore it’s taken.
5) The concentration of growth factors is higher in plasma as compared to the blood.
6) Growth factor has the potential to heal tissues and repair the same.
7) Vampire facial helps in improving the texture, fine lines, and texture of the skin. Moreover, it’s used for acne, surgical scars, fine wrinkles, and melasma.
8) While carrying out this procedure our team of experts does venipuncture (Obtain Blood), It’s painless except for the first needle pinch.
9) After this centrifuge process is done to isolate the platelets.
10) Thereafter, micro needling or microdermabrasion processes occur.
11) This advanced technique is quite beneficial for improving the quality of the skin.
